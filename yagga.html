Yummy Chocolate Brownie Recipe

Time Duration: Less than 1 Hour!


What you'll need:

  • 5 tablespoons of butter
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla
  • 1/3 cup of oil
  • A human soul
  • 3/4 cup of cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup of flour
  • 1 tablespoon of cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • The regrets of the past
  • 3/4 cups of choco chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 325 degrees
  2. Mix up all the ingredients
  3. Put mixture in baking tray
  4. Insert tray into over for 500 minutes
  5. Pray.
